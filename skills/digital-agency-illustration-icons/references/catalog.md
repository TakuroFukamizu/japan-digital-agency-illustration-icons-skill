# Asset catalog

Use `scripts/asset_tool.py search <query>` for ranked semantic search. This guide explains the naming scheme and illustration meanings.

## Icons

Each icon concept has `line` and `fill` SVG files plus 24, 48, and 72 px PNG files. Address an icon as `icon/<name>`, for example `icon/tax`.

Administrative concepts include application, arrival, authentication, bank account, certification, certification with seal, child, code reader, departure, documents, e-application, expenditure, family, fast track, fillable card, health, house, immunization, income, invoice, itinerary, laws, luggage, mailing, medicine, money, mother and child, municipality, pension, privacy protection, public offering, seal certificate, specialist, stamp, tax, transactions, and work.

System concepts include add, arrows, attention, complete, copy, download, help, history, inbox, information, menu, new window, notification, password, personal computer, printer, search, smartphone, and update.

## Illustrations

Address an illustration as `illustration/<filename-without-.png>`.

### Large

- `l_01_rectangle_white`, `l_01_square_white`: people using a digital public service together.
- `l_02_rectangle_white`, `l_02_square_white`: overhead view of a smartphone and a card.

### Medium

- `m_01_*`: card and smartphone with completion check.
- `m_02_*`: hand holding a smartphone with completion check.
- `m_03_*`: smartphone warning or error.
- `m_04_*`: smartphone maintenance or settings.
- `m_05_*`: smartphone displaying a QR code.
- `m_06_*`: scanning or reading a QR code.
- `m_07_*`: smartphone and identity card.
- `m_08_*`: smartphone at a reader, kiosk, or terminal.
- `m_09_*`: hand holding an identity card.
- `m_10_*`: person using a laptop.
- `m_11_*`: person at a service counter.
- `m_12_*`: one person handing a card to another.
- `m_13_*`: person comparing a face with an ID portrait.
- `m_14_*`: person making a smartphone/card-terminal payment.

Replace `*` with `white` for a white/transparent presentation or `warmgray` for the supplied warm-gray panel.

### Small objects and documents

- `s_01`: My Number/identity card front.
- `s_02`: identity card back with IC chip and QR code.
- `s_03`: health insurance card.
- `s_04`, `s_09`: passport cover.
- `s_05`: open passport.
- `s_06`: passport identity page.
- `s_07`: simple identity card front.
- `s_08`: simple card back.
- `s_10`: document or form.
- `s_11`: card in a holder or case.
- `s_12`, `s_13`: smartphone body variants.
- `s_14`: smartphone screen/detail.

### Small hands and people

- `s_hand01`: hand holding a phone horizontally.
- `s_hand02`, `s_hand03`: hand holding a phone vertically, with varied skin tones.
- `s_hand04`: pointing or tapping hand.
- `s_human01`–`s_human18`: diverse head-and-shoulders people; inspect visually to choose a suitable composition.
- `s_human19`–`s_human24`: diverse full-body people; inspect visually to choose a suitable composition.

People assets are components for explanatory compositions, not identity labels. Do not infer or assign ethnicity, gender identity, disability, occupation, or other sensitive attributes from appearance.
